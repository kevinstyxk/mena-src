What are the obstacles for US companies?

The lack of free trade core agreements within the MENA region ceases every country to have its own economic borders, making it very difficult on businesses.
Many US companies are reluctant to work within the MENA region, considering it too risky.
Complicated logistics are a major obstacle for US companies dealing MENA countries.
Import regulations and certifications are different for each country and quite complicated in the larger markets of MENA.
Warrantee and other issues are either obstacles that prevent each US companies from succeeding in the MENA region.
